FAMILY ANCHOR (protected, non-negotiable) / RECOVERY MARGIN (transition/rest — counts as real).

Then flag any IMPOSSIBLE DAYS: too many high-focus blocks back to back,
no recovery margins, or a family anchor in conflict.
